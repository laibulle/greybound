---
title: Models
description: Amp and pedal model targets, separated from reusable circuit-component knowledge.
---

Model pages describe musical targets: a named amp family, pedal family, or rig-facing device. They may reference circuit components, but they should not become reusable component documentation.

Use:

- `models/amps/` for amp families and variants.
- `models/pedals/` for pedal families grouped by category.
- `circuits/` for reusable component cells, passive networks, validation rules, and shared solver behavior.

This separation keeps component engineering reusable while letting each model page explain the musical target, topology choice, controls, validation state, and variant assumptions.
