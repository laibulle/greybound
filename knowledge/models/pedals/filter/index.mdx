---
title: Filter Pedals
description: Envelope-controlled and manually swept filter model targets.
---

Filter pedals change tone by moving resonant cutoff or band-pass regions. In Boutique59 they are distinct from modulation pedals because the movement can be driven by guitar dynamics rather than by an LFO.

Current models:

- [Muon](./muon): envelope filter inspired by the Mu-Tron III family.
