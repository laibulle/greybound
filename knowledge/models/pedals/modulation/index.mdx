---
title: Modulation
description: Modulation effect models and validation targets.
---

Modulation models should make time-varying control state explicit while preserving source/load behavior at their chain boundaries.

Current models:

- Dartford: Vox/Jennings-inspired amp tremolo with rounded LFO motion, subtle band movement, and FX-loop placement.
- Tron: Mu-Tron Phasor II-inspired optical-style six-stage phaser with smoothed lamp/LDR modulation, feedback, and wet/dry mix.
- Jetstream: A/DA Flanger-inspired BBD-style flanger with short modulated delay, manual delay, feedback, and wet/dry mix.
- Celeste: Boss CE-2-inspired BBD-style chorus with dual modulated delay voices, tone shaping, and wet/dry mix.
