---
title: Overdrive
description: Overdrive pedal models and validation targets.
---

Overdrive models should preserve picking dynamics and source/load behavior while adding controlled clipping, EQ, and level shaping.

Current models:

- Minotaur: buffered clean/drive-blend overdrive inspired by mythic transparent-drive circuits.
- Monarch: dual-stage soft-clip overdrive with asymmetric clipping and tone shaping.
