This section contains reusable circuit-component knowledge: cells, passive networks, solver rules, validation gates, and shared electrical behavior. Product or family targets belong under `knowledge/models/`.

## Policy

Do not store copied schematic scans, service PDFs, or current-production circuit artwork in this repository. In this section, document:

- reusable component cells,
- generic topology patterns,
- solver assumptions,
- state variables,
- validation plans,
- public source URLs for readers to consult themselves.

When a circuit source is copyrighted or has unclear redistribution rights, link to it and summarize only the engineering facts needed by Boutique59.

## Current Component Areas

- Passive networks: tone stacks, coupling, loading, and cable behavior.
- Tube stages: ECC83/12AX7 stages, phase inverters, power stages, supply sag.
- BJT stages: transistor pedal cells, diode clipping, coupling capacitors.
- Validation: shared evidence gates for component-level work.

## Confidence Vocabulary

- `high`: stable topology or value across trusted references, or measured in our own fixtures.
- `medium`: plausible public-reference value, but variant-sensitive or not yet measured.
- `low`: implementation placeholder or modeling assumption needing measurement.

Use this vocabulary when adding new circuit notes so implementation choices remain auditable.
